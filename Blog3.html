My Journey in Information Technology

Published on November 10, 2024

Hello! I’m Josh Gerald C. Merzo, a 21-year-old Information Systems student at the Technological University of the Philippines-Manila. I want to share the journey that sparked my passion for technology and the thrill of solving problems through programming.

How It All Began

It all started during my first year in college when I was introduced to programming. Although I found it challenging at first, something clicked as I worked through my first project. I enjoyed the logic, the structure, and the creativity that coding brought. Since then, I’ve been on an exciting journey, learning various programming languages and expanding my technical skills.

Over the past few years, I’ve developed a working knowledge of several programming languages, including C, C++, and Java. Each language has its quirks and strengths, and diving into them has been like learning new dialects of a shared language. My current focus is on improving my proficiency in Java, Databases and exploring web development fundamentals.

Looking ahead, I’m excited to continue building my skills and exploring the latest technology trends. My goal is to become a skilled software developer who can create meaningful, impactful solutions. I’m particularly interested in areas like software engineering, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence.

Thanks for reading, and I hope you’ll follow along as I continue to document my experiences and growth in this field!
